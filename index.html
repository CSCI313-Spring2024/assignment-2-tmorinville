About Me

Hello! My name is Taya Morinville and I am from the small town of Brooks, MN. Currently, I am a senior at NDSU studying Computer Science on the standard track. After graduating with my degree, I hope to pursue a career in Software Development. Within my career, I hope to be able to bring back my knowledge of Computer Science to my small community. Growing up in a small community I did not have much exposure software development, which is why I am passionate about bringing back my knowledge to my community so others can learn about this great field and the opportunities it brings.
